# HeadshotAI - Professional AI Headshots

## Why This Will Make Money
- Proven market: PhotoAI, HeadshotPro, Aragon charge $29-99
- Clear pain point: people need professional photos, hate photoshoots
- High perceived value: "professional headshot" vs "QR code"
- Target: job seekers, LinkedIn users, founders, remote workers

## Pricing
- Single headshot: $5
- Pack of 10: $19
- Unlimited monthly: $29

## User Acquisition Path
1. **Week 1 (0→100 users)**
   - Product Hunt launch
   - Post to r/SideProject, r/InternetIsBeautiful, r/LinkedIn
   - Twitter/X threads showing before/after
   - Hacker News Show HN

2. **Week 2 (100→500 users)**
   - SEO: "AI headshot generator", "professional headshot online"
   - LinkedIn content targeting job seekers
   - Cold outreach to career coaches, resume services

3. **Week 3-4 (500→1000+ users)**
   - Affiliate program for career coaches
   - Integration with resume builders
   - Retargeting ads if budget allows

## Tech Stack
- Next.js + TypeScript + shadcn (same as CraftKit)
- Gemini API for image generation
- Stripe for payments
- Vercel for hosting

## Revenue Target
- 200 users × $25 avg = $5,000 MRR
- Need 7 paying users/day for 30 days
